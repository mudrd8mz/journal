## Dwarf Therapist ##

* games-util/dwarf-therapist - from anders-larsson overlay

Version compatibility controlled by /etc/portage/package.accept_keywords

https://github.com/Dwarf-Therapist/Dwarf-Therapist/releases

## DFHack ##

Download a release from https://github.com/DFHack/dfhack/releases matching the
DF version, architecture and GCC version.

Extract and move the files into ~/.dwarf-fortress

Run ./dfhack there.
